MONSANTO

Annual Report

Monsanto is the world’s largest agricultural company and the leading poioneer in genetic research that continues to shape the future of our food. Monsanto helps feed millions of people on a daily basis, but its public image is tainted by controversy and a growing awareness of GMO foods.

Softened tones of the company’s colors fill out the color scheme with a bright yellow to make them less intense and more natural. Farming is a tradition, and etched agricultural forms join the illustrated etching of Monsanto’s logo to create the feeling of time-honored craft and care. Humans are used whenever possible in photographs, connecting further the idea that Monsanto is a company of people, not profit, and big, bold type helps to convey the enormous global scope of the company’s overall vision.
