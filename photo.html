Photography

I have been attracted to the power of photography all my life, and initially went to school for photojournalism. These photographs are from a series of class projects undertaken to better understand using light and color.

Dumpster Rainbow, above, was the product of a photography assignment to capture beauty in ugliness. The associations we have with what we value and what we don’t– and what we need and what we are done with– eventually result in a pile of trash somewhere. Through the lens, the collective garbage becomes something more mysterious and attractive.

Jeff, left and below, is a portrait series of a former classmate with some interesting hobbies. Capturing the personality of another individual in a few photos can be a daunting task, however, so it made sense to emphasis the oddity and the atmosphere Jeff embraces.
