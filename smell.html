FAMILY SOAP

Packaging

Designed for a fictional company, Kleen Soap, Grandma and Grandpa Smell are two of the first in a line of soaps developed to stimulate your sense of smell as well as your memories.

Our olfactory system can detect at least one trillion different odors, providing mankind with an amazing nasal device. It is also one of the five senses most heavily-connected to our memories, and smells are often among the strongest mental ssociations formed when capturing time, place and people. The connection to past loved ones lives on in their aromas.

The packages are playfully illustrated to provide endearing details of Grandma’s lace or Grandpa’s curduroy, the loving wrinkles around their eyes and their mutual fondness for cleanliness and stature.
