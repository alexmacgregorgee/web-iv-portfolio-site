COLOR of CHANGE

Ad/Activism

Color of Change is a nonprofit organization focused on electing people of color in local, state and national government elections. This project was a call to action against for-profit prisons.

In addition to their main focus, the nonprofit also addresses many other social issues that affect minority lives. Perhaps none has loomed larger in recent past nor casts a darker shadow into the future than the private prison industry.

The emphasis of this awareness campaign begins and ends where the incentive exists for powerful people to trade in human lives: money. The horrifying nature of the private prison system and the social stigmas surrounding law often get in the way of the simple details, like profits and growth. The black and red evokes financial basics, while the black and white photography keeps the racial aspects of this problem on full display.
