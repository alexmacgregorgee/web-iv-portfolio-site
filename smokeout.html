SMOKEOUT SYSTEMS

Website

SmokeOut Systems is a fictional enterprise which sells electronic cigarettes to assist those trying to quit smoking. Unlike the majority of electronic cigarettes flooding the markets in recent years, SmokeOut Systems is one of the first specifically designed for the purpose of smoking cessation. For this reason, it needed to be presented differently than other electronic cigarettes.

A cool, soothing color scheme was chosen to allow the polished mechanical elements of the electronic cigarette to come across as attractively modern and high tech. The focus of the business and the website, the electronic cigarette, fills the page and dominates the eye.


By enlarging the product to ridiculous proportions, the background is able to serve as pure, balancing empty space. Much like the product it was designed to sell, the website is minimal, functional and attractive.

Despite the shifting layouts caused by varying display sizes, the main image of the product remains visually interesting and easy to recognize. Though the growth of e-cigarettes has increased in recent years, many first-time customers are still very hesitant and unsure about the new technology. With this site, they can effectively hold their new SmokeOut System in their hand before purchasing it.
